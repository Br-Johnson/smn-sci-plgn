# Deterministic Workflow Gap

The repo can now route, fetch, and validate a stock-brief contract, but it still does not produce reproducible multi-source workflows at the level of the Life Science Research plugin's stronger composites.

## Why it stays open

- the stock-brief scaffold is still a contract layer, not a full orchestration engine
- provenance weighting is still narrative
- evidence reconciliation is not yet a shared contract
- source skills are still mostly atomic wrappers
